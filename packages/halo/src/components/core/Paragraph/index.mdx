## Usage

<Playground>
  <div className="max-w-lg">
    <InterfaceHeader section flow title="Comfortable, contrast AAA" />
    <Paragraph contrast="AAA">
      Suitable for long, continuous written content, such as editorial or blog
      posts. Applies a natural document flow bottom margin by default.
    </Paragraph>
    <Paragraph contrast="AAA">
      This is following sample paragraph to illustrate the natural bottom margin
      flow.
    </Paragraph>
    <Paragraph contrast="AAA" flow={false}>
      This paragraph has no flow.
    </Paragraph>
    <Paragraph contrast="AAA" flow={false}>
      This is the second of two paragraphs without flow.
    </Paragraph>
  </div>
</Playground>
